How This Dividend Reinvestment Calculator Works

This free DRIP dividend calculator pulls live price and dividend data for any stock or ETF. Enter your ticker symbol and number of shares to instantly see your projected dividend income — broken down by month, quarter, and full year — with compounding reinvestment factored in.

Popular tickers our users calculate: SCHD dividend calculator, JEPQ dividend calculator, MSTY dividend calculator, JEPI, QYLD, SVOL, O (Realty Income), and hundreds more. Monthly-paying ETFs are fully supported with accurate compounding.

The DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment Plan) calculation compounds your income monthly — each month's dividend buys new fractional shares, which then earn dividends the following month. This snowball effect is how long-term dividend investors grow their income over time without adding new capital.

Use the Multi-Year tab to project your dividend income growth over 1 to 30 years. Use the Fill My Calendar tab to find stocks that pay dividends in your weakest income months.
